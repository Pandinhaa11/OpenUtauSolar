About the Project

OpenUtau Solar is a modification of the OpenUtau software that aims to enhance user experience with a bright, solar-inspired, and futuristic interface. It maintains compatibility with standard voicebanks, facilitating music creation.

The main goal is to make vocal synthesis accessible and enjoyable by combining functionality and aesthetics for musicians and digital producers.
